# Future Features & Ideas Roadmap

This document serves as a "Brainstorming Vault" for the next phase of the Droid Hunter ecosystem.

## 🏆 Event Leaderboards & Recognition

- **Event Podium**: 
  - Automatically calculate the **Most Spotted Droid** and **Most Commended Builder** for specific events.
  - If >10 builders are present, display a **Top 3 Leaderboard** on the Portal's event page.

## 🎖️ Gamification (Hunter Rewards)

- **Specific Collection Sets**: 
  - "The Astromech Collector": Scan 5 different R2-series droids.
  - "The Galactic Tourist": Scan droids from 3 different counties.
- **Streaks**: 
  - "Weekend Warrior": Scan droids on two consecutive days of a multi-day event.

## 📸 Content & Community

- **Droid Comments**: 
  - Let hunters leave "Nice work!" or "Amazing dome lights!" comments that builders can read on their private portal.
- **Builder Stories**: 
  - Link the "Back Story" more prominently in the Hunter app so hunters can read the lore while standing in front of the droid.

## 🛠️ Technical Evolutions

- **Offline Mode Persistence**: 
  - Improve the PWA to queue scans if the event venue has poor signal, automatically syncing when the user leaves the building.
- **AR Encounters**: 
  - An experimental AR view where scanning a droid unlocks a floating 3D blueprint or "hologram" of its internal components.

## 🛡️ Security & Integrity

- **Rate Limiting**: 
  - Harder limits on commendations per IP/device to prevent automated vote-stuffing for leaderboards.
